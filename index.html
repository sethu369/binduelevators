Who We Are

Reliable Elevator Services Since 2016

Bindu Elevators is a dedicated elevator service provider based in Andhra Pradesh. Since 2016, we have been handling installation, maintenance, and repair work with a practical and hands-on approach.

The business is led by an experienced professional with over 15 years of experience, having worked as an electrician and lift operator in different companies. We operate with a small team of 3–4 members focused on safe and reliable service.

We do not manufacture elevator components. We use quality parts sourced from trusted suppliers across India including Hyderabad, Gujarat, and Chennai.
