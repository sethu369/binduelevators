Who We Are

Reliable Elevator Services Since 2016

Bindu Elevators is a dedicated elevator service provider based in Andhra Pradesh. Since 2016, we have been handling installation, maintenance, and repair work with a practical and hands-on approach.

The business is led by an experienced professional with over 15 years of experience, having worked as an electrician and lift operator in different companies. We operate with a small team of 3–4 members focused on safe and reliable service.

We do not manufacture elevator components. We use quality parts sourced from trusted suppliers across India including Hyderabad, Gujarat, and Chennai.

Frequently Asked Questions

A standard passenger elevator installation typically takes 4–8 weeks from site clearance, depending on floors and complexity. Home lifts can be done in 2–3 weeks. We provide a detailed project timeline before work begins.
Our AMC covers 8 preventive maintenance visits/year (2/quarter), 24/7 emergency support, lubrication of all moving parts, safety device testing, and minor adjustments. Major component replacements are billed separately with prior approval.
All elevators comply with IS:14665 (Indian Standards for lifts), National Building Code 2016, and CE marking requirements. We also assist with mandatory government inspection certificates from the state electrical inspectorate.
Yes. We maintain and repair elevators from OTIS, Schindler, Kone, ThyssenKrupp, and local manufacturers. Our engineers are trained across multiple brands and control systems.
Our low-pit home lift solutions require a minimum pit depth of just 150mm (6 inches), making them ideal for retrofitting in existing homes. Standard installations require 450–600mm. We assess your site during the free consultation.
1-year comprehensive warranty on all new installations (parts & labour). Motor and traction machine carry a 3-year warranty. Control panels are warranted for 2 years. Extended warranty packages available via AMC agreements.
